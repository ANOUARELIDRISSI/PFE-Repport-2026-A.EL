Document Lifecycle State Model

A document becomes retrievable evidence only after acquisition, normalization, quality gating, extraction, and chunk indexing have completed.

1. Acquired

Raw document stored
source URL, title, date, source name, scraped metadata

2. Normalized

Clean text and metadata aligned
HTML/PDF noise removed; fields mapped to a common schema

3. Accepted

Quality and relevance gate passed
low-quality or non-political documents are skipped

4. Enriched

Entities, relations, and records extracted
graph facts and structured records are produced

5. Indexed

Chunks embedded and linked to entities
document_chunks and ZVec vectors support retrieval
