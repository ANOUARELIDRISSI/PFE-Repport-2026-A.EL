Reasoning Modes

Three retrieval strategies: strict RAG, chain-of-thought retrieval, and reflexive evidence audit.

RAG

Single retrieval pass using the original question only.

Original question
Retrieve vector chunks
Synthesize cited answer

Chain of Thought

Planning and follow-up searches extend the reasoning chain.

Plan sub-questions
Retrieve chunks + graph facts
Choose next entities or topics
Combine chain into answer

Reflexive

An audit step decides whether evidence is sufficient before synthesis.

Plan research path
Retrieve evidence + graph relations
Evidence sufficient?
Search missing evidence
Final synthesis
