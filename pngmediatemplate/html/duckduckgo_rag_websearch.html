When it runs: user enables Web search, a reflexive audit needs external evidence, the question signals recency (latest, breaking...), or an administrator launches topic discovery. Web results stay separate from the local corpus in the LLM context, then fuse into one cited answer.

Internal RAG remains the primary path; DuckDuckGo adds supplementary evidence until the same sources are ingested into the platform.
